Features four key systems:

- ❄️ **Automatic Cooling System**  
- 🔥 **Firefighting System**  
- 🌡️ **Automated Air Conditioning System**  
- 🔒 **Security System** for intruder detection  

---

## 🚪 Automatic Entry Detection System

Detects customers approaching using an **ultrasonic sensor** and automatically opens/closes the door with a **servo motor** for seamless and secure entry.

> **Components:**  
> - ⚙️ Servo Motor  
> - 📡 Ultrasonic Sensor  

![Automatic Entry Detection](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f86acbed-ca17-4638-957b-704dc8fc865c)

---

## 🔥 Firefighting System

Detects fire using a **flame sensor** and activates a **buzzer** and LCD warning. An **AC water pump** controlled via **relay** extinguishes the fire efficiently.

> **Components:**  
> - 🔥 Flame Sensor  
> - 🔔 Buzzer  
> - 🔌 Relay  
> - 💧 AC Water Pump  

![Firefighting System](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8c1ad3f7-ac2c-4030-a00f-9a05861aa29b)

---

## 🌬️ Automated Air Conditioning System

Monitors temperature and humidity with a **DHT11 sensor** and activates a 12V fan when thresholds are exceeded. Data is displayed on an LCD.

> **Components:**  
> - 🌡️ DHT11 Temperature & Humidity Sensor  
> - 🌀 12V Fan  
> - 📺 LCD with I2C  

![Automated Air Conditioning System](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a0c8a47a-d772-433e-973a-89955d4440b0)
